Privacy Policy

Mosque App

Effective Date: October 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mosque App, a comprehensive service hub platform designed to connect and serve the Muslim community. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Mosque App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We encourage you to read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Mosque affiliation and community details
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

2.2 Technical Information

  • Device information and operating system
  • App usage analytics and performance data
  • IP address and general location information
  • Log files and crash reports

3. App Permissions and Data Usage

Our app requests specific permissions to provide enhanced functionality. Below are the permissions we request and how we use them:

📷 Camera and Gallery Access

Purpose: To allow users to upload profile photos and document religious events, community activities, and mosque-related content.

Data Usage: Photos are stored securely on our servers and are only accessible to authorized users within your mosque community. Images are compressed and optimized for storage efficiency.

User Control: You can revoke camera access at any time through your device settings. Existing photos can be deleted from your profile.

📍 Location Services

Purpose: To provide precise location tracking for mosque events, prayer time calculations based on your location, and to help users find nearby mosques and Islamic centers.

Data Usage: Location data is used to enhance your experience with accurate prayer times, event notifications, and community discovery. We do not track your location continuously.

User Control: Location sharing is optional and can be disabled in app settings or device permissions at any time.

📁 File Storage Access

Purpose: To enable mosque organizations to upload verification documents during registration and to allow users to share religious content, event flyers, and educational materials.

Data Usage: Uploaded files are scanned for security, stored encrypted, and are only accessible to authorized mosque administrators for verification purposes.

User Control: You control what files you upload and can request deletion of your uploaded content at any time.

💳 Payment Processing (Stripe Integration)

Purpose: To facilitate secure donations, event registrations, and other mosque-related transactions through our trusted payment partner, Stripe.

Data Usage: Payment information is processed directly by Stripe and is not stored on our servers. We only receive transaction confirmations and basic payment metadata.

User Control: All payment methods can be managed through your account settings. You can remove saved payment methods at any time.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining our app services
  • Facilitating communication within mosque communities
  • Processing donations and event registrations
  • Sending prayer time notifications and event reminders
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations

5. Data Protection and Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Security Measures Include:
  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with regular backups
  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Compliance with industry-standard security protocols
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our security measures and will notify users of any significant security incidents.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (like Stripe for payments) that help us operate our app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users and the public
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with user notification)

All third-party service providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for the specified purposes.

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have several rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Export your data in a commonly used format

7.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update your profile information at any time through the app
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information

7.3 Deletion and Withdrawal

  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for specific data processing activities
  • Request deletion of specific content or information

7.4 Privacy Controls

  • Manage notification preferences
  • Control visibility of your profile and activities
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Specific retention periods include:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for legal and accounting purposes
  • Communication Logs: Retained for 1 year for support and quality purposes
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for 2 years for service improvement

9. Children's Privacy

Mosque App is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

Parents and guardians who believe their child has provided personal information to us should contact us immediately using the information provided below.

10. International Data Transfers
